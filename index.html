Instructions
Left click to create new points
Right click to select a point. If more than two points are selected, they will be connected with a spring
Middle mouse click to hold a point; drag the mouse to drag the point
Press "p" to toggle drawing of points
Press "s" to toggle drawing of springs
Press "delete" to delete all the springs and points
Press "space" to toggle gravity
Press "h" to bring up the instructions again
Click anywhere to hide the instructions
